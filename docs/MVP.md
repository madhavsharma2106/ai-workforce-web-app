# MVP Scope

## Goal

Validate that business owners will trust an AI employee to generate outbound pipeline every day.

## Included

- Account Manager Employee
- Lead Sourcer employee
- Sales Representative employee
- Daily execution of some employees
- Lead research
- Email drafting
- Sending approved emails from the founder's Outlook account
- Reply monitoring
- Approval queue
- Feedback learning

## Not Included

- Marketplace
- Custom tools
- Billing
- CRM integrations
- Assignment builder
- Complex permissions

This prevents feature creep.
