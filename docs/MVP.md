# MVP Scope

## Goal

Validate that founders will trust an AI employee to generate outbound pipeline every day.

## Included

- Account Manager Employee
- Lead Sourcer employee
- Sales Representative employee
- Daily execution of some employees
- Lead research
- Email drafting
- Sending approved emails from the founder's Outlook account
- Reply monitoring
- Approval queue
- Feedback learning

> **Status:** this is the target MVP scope, not a record of what's built. See [AGENTS.md](AGENTS.md) ("Current status") and [INTEGRATIONS.md](INTEGRATIONS.md) for what actually exists today — as of now, sending approved emails, reply monitoring, and feedback learning are not yet built (no Outlook/Graph integration exists).

## Not Included

- Marketplace
- Custom tools
- Billing
- CRM integrations
- Assignment builder
- Complex permissions

This prevents feature creep.
