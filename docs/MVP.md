# MVP Scope

## Goal

Validate that business owners will trust an AI employee to generate outbound pipeline every day.

## Included

- Lead Sourcer employee
- Daily execution
- Lead research
- Email drafting
- Approval queue
- Feedback learning

## Not Included

- Marketplace
- Multi-agent collaboration
- Prompt editor
- Custom tools
- Billing
- CRM integrations
- Workflow builder
- Complex permissions

This prevents feature creep.
